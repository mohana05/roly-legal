# Wibo — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 3, 2026_

**Languages:** English · [Español](privacy-es) · [Français](privacy-fr) · [Deutsch](privacy-de) · [日本語](privacy-ja)

### The short version
Wibo is a learning game for young children. It does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information about you or your child. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, and no third-party tracking. Everything happens on your device.

### What we collect
Nothing personal. We do not ask for a name, email, photo, location, contacts, or any identifier. We never see who is using the app.

### What is stored on your device
To make the app work, a few small settings are saved **locally on your device only** — such as whether the welcome screens have been seen, which stickers have been earned, and your chosen language, season, and sound preferences. This never leaves your device and is not visible to us or anyone else. You can erase it any time in **Parent Zone → "Delete all data."**

### Voice
Wibo speaks using your device's built-in text-to-speech. No microphone is used, and no audio is recorded or sent anywhere.

### No ads, no analytics, no third parties
Wibo contains no advertising, no analytics or measurement tools, and no third-party software development kits. No data is transmitted off the device.

### Purchases
An optional subscription ("Wibo Plus") is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Apple processes payment; we never receive your payment details. Purchases are started from a parent-only area behind a press-and-hold gate.

### Children's privacy (COPPA & GDPR)
Wibo is designed for children and complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU/UK GDPR, and similar laws. Because we collect no personal information from anyone — including children — there is nothing to disclose, sell, or request consent for.

### Deleting your data
Open Parent Zone (press and hold to enter), then tap **"Delete all data."** This erases all locally-stored progress immediately.

### Changes
If this policy ever changes, the updated version will appear here and in the app.
